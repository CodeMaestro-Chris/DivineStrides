Who we are

About Us

Divine Strides Inc. is a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt, not-for-profit organization based in Maryland, USA. Established to support the needy, impoverished and less privileged, through impactful giving within communities.

We engage in purposeful giving, sourcing essential goods, and distribute them through our network of outreach platforms, supporting people in need to open opportunities for the needy.

What To Know

Our Approach
We partner with various organizations to create opportunities and provide essential resources that improve lives and foster community development.

Our Service Areas
Divine Strides Inc. actively serves communities within Maryland and its surrounding areas, ensuring that resources and support reach those in need.

Our Partnerships

  • Global Health & Environment Foundation Inc. – In Bowie, Maryland and other supporting organizations

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve the quality of life for underprivileged populations by sponsoring comprehensive skill acquisition programs that equip youth with valuable, marketable skills. Through hands-on training, mentorship, and access to essential resources, we empower individuals to become self-reliant, confident, and capable of securing sustainable livelihoods.

By fostering entrepreneurship, innovation, and career development, we not only uplift individuals but also contribute to the broader economic growth of communities.

Learn More

Our Vision

Our vision is to inspire hope and actively contribute to the development more sustainable future for underserved communities. We strive to empower individuals by providing them with access to essential resources, opportunities for growth, and the encouragement needed to overcome challenges.

By fostering resilience and promoting inclusive development, we work toward creating a world where every individual, regardless of their background or circumstances, has the chance to thrive and achieve their full potential.

Our Programs & Services

We engage in purposeful giving, sourcing essential goods, and distribute them through our network of outreach platforms, supporting people in need to open opportunities for the needy.

Skills & Capacity Building

At Divine Strides Inc., we offer training in IT, computer software, and repairs, as well as tutoring and mentoring, in partnership with Joseph Health and Global Services, to help build up independent individuals.

Food & Essential Supplies

Organized through our Pantry & Distribution Center in Silver Spring, providing nutritious meals to struggling families and distributed via our outreach platforms.

Counseling & Support Services

Providing emotional and psychological support to individuals in crisis. As well as connecting those in need with additional services and professional help.

Making A Difference

We are committed to transforming lives by addressing critical needs in underserved communities. Through our programs, partnerships, and dedicated volunteers, education, and support to those facing hardship.
